Healthy Chocolate Banana Bites

These irresistible chocolate banana bites are the perfect guilt-free treat that takes just 15 minutes to make. With only 5 simple ingredients, they're naturally sweetened and packed with wholesome goodness that'll satisfy your sweet tooth without the crash.

Why You'll Love These Chocolate Banana Bites

Let me tell you about the day I discovered these magical little treats! I was craving something sweet but didn't want to derail my healthy eating goals. Instructions

  • Prepare bananas: Peel and slice bananas into thick rounds, about 1/2 inch thick. Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  • Make chocolate mixture: In a small bowl, mix together almond butter, cocoa powder, and maple syrup until smooth and well combined.
  • Assemble bites: Spread a small amount of chocolate mixture on half of the banana slices, then top with remaining banana slices to create sandwiches.
  • Add toppings: Press mini chocolate chips gently into the chocolate filling around the edges of each bite.
  • Chill and serve: Place in refrigerator for 10 minutes to set, then serve immediately or store in an airtight container for up to 3 days.

Recipe Notes

  • Banana ripeness: Use ripe bananas for the best sweetness and flavor. They should be yellow with a few brown spots.
  • Storage: Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. For longer storage, freeze for up to 1 month.
  • Nut-free option: Substitute almond butter with sunflower seed butter or tahini for a nut-free version.
  • Sweetener alternatives: You can use honey, agave, or date syrup instead of maple syrup. Adjust to taste.
  • Make ahead tip: These actually taste better after chilling for a few hours as the flavors meld together beautifully!
