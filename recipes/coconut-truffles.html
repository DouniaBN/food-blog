Ingredients

Instructions

  • Step 1: In a bowl measure out the coconut butter and melt it.
  • Step 2: Pour in the condensed coconut milk and mix with the coconut butter until combined.
  • Step 3: Add in the desiccated coconut and mix.
  • Step 4: Shape into balls — batter will be slightly sticky but malleable.
  • Step 5: Roll each ball in extra desiccated coconut.
  • Step 6: Place in the freezer for 15 minutes until they look set.
  • Step 7: Enjoy!!

Pro Tips

  • Let each ball sit out at room temperature for a few minutes before enjoying.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I store 3-ingredient coconut truffles?

Store the coconut truffles in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. For longer storage, keep them in the freezer for up to 1 month. Allow frozen truffles to sit at room temperature for a few minutes before serving.

Are these healthy coconut truffles completely sugar-free?

You can make these coconut truffles added sugar free by using a sugar free coconut condensed milk. Coconut has naturally occurring sugars which will always be present.

Are coconut truffles gluten free?

Yes, these 3-ingredient coconut truffles are completely gluten free, dairy free and refined sugar free depending on the condensed coconut milk used.

Do coconut truffles need to be refrigerated?

Yes, because these coconut truffles are made with coconut butter and condensed coconut milk, refrigeration helps them firm up and maintain their shape. They can sit at room temperature briefly when serving, but should be stored chilled for best texture.
