No-Bake Mango Yogurt Chocolate Bites

Your new favorite 3-ingredient summer dessert! Refreshing mango and Greek yogurt wrapped in rich dark chocolate - naturally sweetened and ready in just 10 minutes prep.

10 min prep 6 bites Easy Gluten-Free

Recipe by Dounia

Certified Holistic Nutritionist

Recipe Summary

★★★★★
4.9 out of 5 (127 reviews)
Prep Time: 10 minutes
Freeze Time: 2 hours
Total Time: 2 hours 10 minutes
Servings: 6 bites

These no-bake mango yogurt chocolate bites are the perfect summer refresher! With just 3 simple ingredients, you'll create a dessert that's naturally sweetened, protein-packed, and absolutely irresistible. The combination of tropical mango, creamy Greek yogurt, and rich dark chocolate creates the ultimate healthy treat.

Ingredients

Simple, wholesome ingredients for maximum flavor!

Choose a ripe mango that gives slightly to pressure - it should smell fragrant and sweet
Use thick, full-fat Greek yogurt for the best texture. For vegan option, substitute with coconut yogurt
Choose high-quality dark chocolate. For refined sugar-free, opt for stevia-sweetened dark chocolate
Flaky sea salt enhances the chocolate and mango flavors beautifully

Adjust Servings

Recipe scales to 6 bites

Instructions

1

Prepare chocolate base

Melt the dark chocolate using a double boiler or microwave in 30-second intervals, stirring between each interval. Pour melted chocolate into your silicone molds or ice cube trays, covering the bottom evenly. Flash freeze for 10 minutes until set.

Tip: Make sure chocolate is completely melted and smooth for the best coating
2

Prepare mango yogurt mixture

In a medium bowl, combine Greek yogurt and diced mango. Gently fold together until evenly mixed, keeping mango chunks intact for texture.

Tip: Don't overmix - you want visible mango pieces for bursts of flavor
3

Assemble the bites

Remove molds from freezer. Add 2-3 teaspoons of the mango yogurt mixture to each mold on top of the chocolate base, filling about 3/4 full.

Tip: Leave room at the top for the final chocolate layer
4

Add chocolate top

Drizzle or spread the remaining melted chocolate over the yogurt layer to create the top, sealing the edges. Sprinkle with a pinch of flaky sea salt.

Tip: The sea salt elevates the chocolate and mango flavors - don't skip it!
5

Freeze and serve

Freeze for 2 hours or until completely set throughout. Remove from molds by gently pressing the bottom or twisting silicone molds. Serve immediately for best texture.

Tip: Let them sit at room temperature for 1-2 minutes before serving for easier eating

Recipe Notes & Tips

Make it Vegan

Substitute Greek yogurt with coconut yogurt for a dairy-free version. Coconut pairs beautifully with mango and creates an equally creamy texture.

Storage

Refrigerator: Up to 3 days
Freezer: Up to 1 week
Store in airtight container with parchment between layers.

Mold Options

Silicone molds work best for easy removal. You can also use ice cube trays, mini muffin tins with liners, or chocolate molds for different shapes.

Ingredient Swaps

Mango: Can use frozen (thawed and drained)
Chocolate: Use any % cocoa you prefer
Yogurt: Any thick, plain yogurt works
